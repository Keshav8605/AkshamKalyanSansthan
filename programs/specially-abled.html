Disability Support

Empowering Abilities, Enabling Independence

Disability is not inability. We provide mobility aids, assistive devices, and support that give specially abled individuals the freedom to live with dignity.

About This Program

Mobility is Freedom, Dignity is Everything

India has over 26 million persons with disabilities, yet only a fraction have access to assistive devices. For many, a simple wheelchair or tricycle means the difference between confinement and independence.

We identify individuals with locomotor disabilities in rural and peri-urban areas, assess their specific needs, procure custom-fitted mobility aids (tricycles, wheelchairs, crutches, prosthetics), and provide them along with physiotherapy guidance and counseling for families.

Our Process

How We Enable Independence

01

Identification & Assessment

Our field workers identify persons with disabilities through community referrals, government data, and grassroots networks. A physiotherapist assesses each individual's specific requirements.

02

Custom Device Procurement

Based on assessment, we procure the right type and size of assistive device — be it a motorized tricycle, a folding wheelchair, crutches, or hearing aids — from certified manufacturers.

03

Distribution & Training

Each device is personally handed over in a respectful ceremony. We train the recipient and family members on proper usage, maintenance, and safe operation of the device.

04

Follow-up & Livelihood Support

Our team follows up quarterly for device maintenance. Where possible, we connect recipients with skill development programs, government schemes, and livelihood opportunities.

Why This Matters

Dignity and Inclusion for All

Only 5% of disabled persons in India have access to assistive devices. The rest live confined, dependent, and often forgotten.

  • From Confinement to Freedom

    A tricycle enables a person to move independently, access markets, schools, and healthcare — transforming their entire life trajectory.

  • Economic Independence

    With mobility, disabled persons can work, earn, and contribute — reducing family dependency and building self-worth.

  • Mental Health & Dignity

    Independence restores self-esteem, reduces depression, and enables social participation that every human being deserves.
